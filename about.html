About KLE Society

It was in the year 1916, a humble effort and modest beginning made by seven dedicated teachers to provide basic education to the economically and educationally deprived knowledge seeker that transformed itself into the establishment of the now globally known K.L.E. Society. These noble souls are worshipfully reversed by KLE family as the ‘SAPTARSHIS’. Since then, unremittingly the Society has been performing a tireless mission in the academic and socio-economic upliftment of the people of North Karnataka region. The century long saga has proven track records of many historical and illustrious milestones. The year 2016 is to be written in golden letters that, when will be celebrating 100 years of Society’s momentous fruitful functioning as an ICON of the academic world. K.L.E. Society is imparting education from KG to PG to Research. Now the horizon has widened with expansion of institutions at international level too.
